A lightweight, serious English proficiency test you can complete in 10 minutes.

How does it work?

For each question, there are two choices. Choose the one that is the better English.

How long does it take?

A typical test is about 8 minutes and 40 questions, but your test may be longer or shorter.

What does it measure?

A higher score indicates stronger English language skills. Scores range from 20 to 120.

Why?

Test24 (working name) is designed to be an accurate, organically secure and very quick measure of basic English ability for both English learners and native English speakers.
